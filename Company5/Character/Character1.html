Princess Hibana

Princess Hibana, a brown, voluptuous woman with shoulder-length light-pink hair and blue eyes with pink flower-shaped pupils, is dressed in a v-shaped white dress with a gold-buckled belt, whip, and holster, as well as dark high heels, a golden necklace, and a personalized fur-trim jacket with blue lines. She also wears a light pullover with woolen sleeves, a vest, a black skirt, and tights, all of which have patches with the number 5 denoting her association with the 5th Special Fire Brigade. In addition, she carries an iron fan on the battlefield. Throughout the series, she evolves from a power-hungry and arrogant character to one driven by empathy and a sense of responsibility. She shifts her focus from personal gain to serving the greater good,She is looking for redemption for her past actions. Through her journey, she discovers the importance of teamwork and becomes an invaluable member of the Fire Force, representing themes of redemption and self-discovery..

Abilities: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Hibana, a Third Generation pyrokinetic, uses the "Clematis" talent to create flower-shaped flames with her hands or a fan. These flames resemble cherry blossoms and may injure opponents when touched, easily destroying numerous Infernal cores. She manipulates the surrounding temperature, extinguishing some Ignition Abilities and triggering heat syncope in opponents, resulting in dizziness. With her scientific understanding, she evaluates opponents' abilities and devises heat control strategies to counter their strikes.
