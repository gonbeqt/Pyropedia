Toru Kishiri

Tōru Kishiri, with his shaggy blonde hair and tendency for chewing bubblegum, makes a unique figure in his bunker suit covered with blue linings. Initially a devoted member of the White-Clad, his battle prowess is obvious. However, as the story progresses, glimpses of his background and internal tensions surface, testing his loyalties. Through a delicate redemption arc, he examines his convictions and eventually seeks atonement. Though not central, Kishiri's evolution contributes to the series' study of redemption and justice.

Abilities: Second Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Tōru, a Third Generation, causes explosions by inflating bubblegum balloons with explosive gas. He is resourceful on the battlefield, finding cover to assault without risking injury from gunshots.
