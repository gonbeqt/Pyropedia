Sho Kusakabe

Shō, a young teenager with blazing red eyes and pristine white hair, resembles an angel. He wears a White-Clad outfit and wields a katana. After quitting the White-Clad, he dresses in showy civilian attire. Shō, who was initially emotionally distant and apathetic to his family, now commands respect as Commander of the Knights of the Ashen Flame. He is cool and ready to kill without hesitation. However, reconnecting with Shinra and his lost memories causes emotional maturation, including tears and curiosity about the outside world. Shō's Adolla Burst ignited mysteriously, resulting in his kidnapping by Haumea and believing in the Evangelist as his parent.

Abilities: Fourth Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Shō's Fourth Generation pyrokinetic ability allows him to manipulate time and draw heat from the expanding universe. He no longer needs Grace during the Cataclysm because of the enhanced Adolla bond.Shō is a skilled swordsman who wields his katana with accuracy and uses the flat side to incapacitate opponents without killing them. Despite his brother's pyrokinetic abilities, Shō easily defeats him in combat.His incredible speed lets him to stay up with fast opponents like Shinra and Joker, even when his time manipulation powers are negated. Shō's extraordinary strength allows him to easily launch Shinra across rooms and destroy solid structures.
