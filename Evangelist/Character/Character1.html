Evangelist

The Evangelist is a mysterious, hooded creature with a diabolical look, including vacant eye sockets and star-like sparks within. She wears a light-colored robe with two horns sprouting from her hood. She holds enormous strength and is related with the Sun God's emblem.She persuaded Sumire Sugita of the importance of the Great Cataclysm and oversaw its unsuccessful effort. She uses religion to deceive survivors and formed the Holy Sol Temple with her disciple Yona, who imitates Raffles Smith. She organizes the Second Cataclysm and gathers disciples such as Faerie and Dragon for the promised day. The Evangelist is knowledgeable about spontaneous human combustion, Adolla Burst, and the Adolla world. She kidnaps children for research, which results in the acquisition of Haumea as the Second Pillar. She appears in pivotal moments, causing Mari Kusakabe to be blinded and influencing Shō Kusakabe's destiny..

Abilities:

The Evangelist has remarkable skills, including going through the Tear in Space, generating earthquakes, and inducing severe pain in people who gaze straight at her. She can provide pyrokinetic skills known as Fourth Generation to individuals with the Adolla Link, known as Grace, which she may forfeit at anytime..
