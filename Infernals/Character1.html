Regular Infernal

Regular infernals in Fire Force are mindless, fiery beings with dark charcoal skin caused by spontaneous human combustion. Past wants may influence their conduct or cause them to remain in certain regions. Regardless of the danger, physically strong people can temporarily restrain them. Hellhounds, or infernal animals, also exist. The Fire Force Brigade is dedicated to extinguishing these infernals and protecting the safety of the living by laying their souls to rest.

Abilities: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

Leonard's Voltage Nova Ignition Ability allows him to improve his physical powers by igniting flames within his body, increasing his firepower over time. He also Master's Hand to Hand Combat with Immense Durability (3 stages), and Immense Strength (4 stages).

Demon Infernal

Demon Infernals are a formidable subtype in Fire Force, distinguished by increased offense, durability, and prominent horns on their heads. They present serious obstacles to the Fire Force, necessitating special attention and tactical tactics in combat, and play an important role in the storyline.

Sentient Infernal

A person converts into a Sentient Infernal with a strong desire to live, they retain self-awareness and control over their flames. Unlike typical Infernals, these special beings have consciousness and intelligence. This complicates the issues faced by the Special Fire Force Company in countering spontaneous human combustion.
