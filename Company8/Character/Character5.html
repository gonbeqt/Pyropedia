Iris

Iris, a young lady with short blonde hair and blue eyes, often wears a habit with a veil, sandals, gloves, stockings, and a cross-shaped brooch. Her clothing features blue lines, indicating her participation in the Special Fire Force. In combat, she wears a neck protector and maybe a gas mask. Even her underwear has the same symbol. During cleaning ceremonies, she wears revealing light attire. In everyday life, Iris prefers classic but charming skirts and blouses. Throughout "Fire Force," Iris grows from a protected nun to a mature and independent person. She analyzes her views, faces hardships, and becomes a source of strength for her companions. Her journey includes reconciling her faith with the harsh facts of the world, demonstrating great emotional resilience and growth.

Abilities: Third Generation (Pyrokinetic)

After the finding of all eight Pillars, Iris developed increased pyrokinetic skills. She can generate little embers and project a protective aura of heat, sheltering herself and companions from flames, akin to Pan Ko Paat's Ignition Ability. This power appears to be linked to Iris' prayers and connection with the Adolla Realm.
