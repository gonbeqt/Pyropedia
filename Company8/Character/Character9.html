Vulcan Joseph

Vulcan Joseph, a skilled engineer in "Fire Force," undergoes profound character development. Initially distrusting of others due to his family's history, he eventually confronts ethical dilemmas and explores his legacy. Through his journey, he discovers his true strengths and contributions to Company 8, evolving into a more confident and principled individual, all while sporting his signature attire: a ram mask, goggles, and animal-themed shirts over his bunker gear.

Abilities: Non-Powered Human

Vulcan's primary expertise is engineering, as seen by his ability to build and maintain Company 8's equipment, including specialized gear such as Maki's Iron Owls. Despite his profession, he is physically fit thanks to intensive training and can participate in missions including both logistical support and warfare
